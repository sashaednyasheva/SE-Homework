About Sasha

Where in Russia I'm from?

I was born in Republic of Kalmykia. Kalmykia is one Russia's four regions where most of the population are Buddhists. Kalmykia is slightly larger than Switzerland and Belgium. Its flag displays a lotus, which is not just a Buddhist symbol here: in summer, thousands of pink lotuses flower in the south of the region.

What Kalmykia is famous for?

If you are a chess player, then don't miss the City of Chess with a museum and giant chess boards. Each spring, the steppes turn into a floral carpet. First, tulips bloom, then - lotuses. If you are a Buddhist, you are in luck as Kalmykia is home to the largest Buddhist temple in Europe, the Golden Abode of the Buddha Shakyamuni.
